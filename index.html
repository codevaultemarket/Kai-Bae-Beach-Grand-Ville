Our Story

A Sanctuary by the Sea

Nestled directly on the shores of Kai Bae Beach — one of Koh Chang's most beloved and serene coastlines — Kai Bae Beach Grand Ville offers an authentic Thai island experience unlike any other. Just steps from the warm, crystalline waters of the Gulf of Thailand, our property is where tropical nature and genuine hospitality meet.

Each of our 17 private rooms and bungalows is fully equipped and thoughtfully designed, many featuring private balconies with unobstructed views of the ocean. Wake up to the gentle sound of waves, watch spectacular sunsets from your terrace, and fall asleep to a sky full of stars.

Located in the heart of Kai Bae village, you are within walking distance of charming restaurants, local markets, beach bars, and adventure tour operators. Yet somehow, it always feels like your own private retreat.
